About Dolmen Studio

Welcome! I'm a passionate web designer and tech problem-solver based right in your neighborhood. With years of experience building custom websites and fixing all sorts of computer issues, I help local businesses and individuals establish their online presence and keep their technology running smoothly.

Whether you need a stunning new website, help with software troubles, or guidance on choosing the right tech solutions, I'm here to make technology work for you, not against you.

What I Do

Web Design

Custom, responsive websites that look great on any device. From simple landing pages to complex business sites, I build with modern standards and accessibility in mind.

Tech Support

Computer running slow? Software not working? I troubleshoot and fix a wide range of technical issues, from hardware repairs to software configuration.

Consulting

Need guidance on technology decisions? I help you choose the right tools, software, and solutions for your specific needs and budget.

Maintenance

Keep your website fresh and secure with regular updates, content changes, and technical maintenance. Peace of mind, included.

SEO Optimization

Get found online! I optimize your website for search engines to help local customers discover your business when they need it most.

Training

Learn to manage your own website or master essential software. I provide patient, one-on-one training tailored to your skill level.
